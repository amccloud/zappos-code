Travel

One does not simply walk through the Zombie Apocalypse. A good pair of running shoes is recommend. You may also want to consider a more robust mode of transportation, like a car.

Food

Abandoned grocery stores are a good place to start your hunt for Twinkies and other non-perishable goods. Don't forget the water, water purification tablets. If fluids are hard to come by you can always Bear Grylls it.

Weapons

You can find weapons almost anywhere. Did you know your local WalMart may have weapons? Ideally you want to destroy the zombie's brain or immobilize them. Be creative and more importantly have fun.

Shelter

If you don't get to high grounds quickly you're gonna have a bad time. If you're lucky you'll find safe houses already stocked with things you need. Just remember to keep the door closed.

Communication

Communicating with potential survivors is about patience and persistence. Use all avenues of communication available to you, update your Twitter feed to let people know you're alive, and make sure to use the #zombie hashtag.

Moving

Rotting zombies will start to build up outside of your stronghold. Keep moving. Ideally you should head for Vermont and find Will Smith.
